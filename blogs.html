Time to shine.!

He wakes up regularly at 6. Then he goes to his office. He has to work to get money. He needs money because he needs to run his family. He does this everyday without changing anything. He doesn't want to do this but he does. Nothing changes.

She wakes up regularly at 5. Prepares breakfast. Does her work like a robot and does nothing. Nothing gets changed, even though she wants a change.

We need change. Everyone wants something new. Everyone wants to change their everyday life. They wants something new. They just want it and get quite and does their everyday work over and over again.

We have to do something new. We are not robots. New things is the key get our mind in our way. We have to make new things. something creative must be done. Once you have cleared the idea of change, once you know how to change and break the usual rule without hesitating, trust me you're free. Just go do. You as you are. Just go and shine.

Trust me, you are ok with this. Just go and shine. It's time to shine.
